Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 02, 2025

Suvojeet Sengupta ("I," "me," or "my") built the NoteNext app as an Open Source app. This Privacy Policy is used to inform you regarding my policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information if anyone decided to use my Service.

By using NoteNext, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The information that I collect is used for providing and improving the Service. I will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

NoteNext is designed with your privacy as a top priority. All of your data, including your notes, checklists, images, and settings, is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any external servers.

a. Information You Provide

We collect the content you create and store within the app, which includes:

  • Notes (titles and content)
  • Checklists
  • Labels
  • Projects
  • Reminders
  • Attachments such as images and other files you add to your notes.
  • Settings, including your theme preferences and app lock PIN (stored securely).

This data resides solely on your device.

b. Permissions

NoteNext requests certain permissions to enable its features. All permissions are used only for the stated purpose within the app's functionality:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To allow you to attach images and other files from your device's storage to your notes.
  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To fetch link previews when you include a URL in your notes and to download Google Fonts for a richer text experience. The app checks for a network connection before attempting to fetch this data.
  • USE_BIOMETRIC: To enable the App Lock feature, allowing you to secure the app with your fingerprint or other biometric credentials.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM & USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT: To set and display precise reminders that you create for your notes.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To show notifications for your reminders.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide and enhance your experience with NoteNext. This includes:

  • Storing, displaying, and organizing your notes and other content.
  • Enabling features like reminders, app lock, and rich link previews.
  • Personalizing your experience through themes and layout settings.

All data processing occurs locally on your device.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

I do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Since all your data is stored locally on your device, I do not have access to it. The only exception is when you voluntarily choose to share a note using the app's "Share" functionality, which uses your device's native sharing capabilities.

4. Third-Party Services

NoteNext uses a few third-party libraries to provide its features:

  • Google Fonts: To provide a better visual experience, the app may download fonts from Google Fonts. This involves a network request to Google's servers.
  • Jsoup: To generate rich link previews, the app makes a network request to the URL you provide in a note.

These services have their own privacy policies, and I encourage you to review them. NoteNext does not send any of your personal note content to these services.

5. Data Security, Retention, and Deletion

I value your trust in providing us your Personal Information. I have implemented the following measures to protect it:

  • Local Storage: All your note data is stored in a private, local database on your device, which is not accessible by other apps.
  • App Lock: You can enable an additional layer of security by setting up a PIN or using biometric authentication to protect access to the app.

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you use the app. You can delete notes at any time, which moves them to the Bin. Notes in the Bin are permanently deleted after a user-configurable period (defaulting to 7 days). Uninstalling the app will also delete all of its data from your device.
