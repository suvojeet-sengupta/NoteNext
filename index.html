Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

Suvojeet Sengupta ("I," "me," or "my") built the NoteNext app as an Open Source app. This Privacy Policy is used to inform you regarding my policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information if anyone decided to use my Service.

By using NoteNext, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The information that I collect is used for providing and improving the Service. I will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

NoteNext is designed with your privacy as a top priority. All of your data, including your notes, checklists, images, and settings, is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any external servers.

a. Information You Provide

We collect the content you create and store within the app, which includes:

  • Notes (titles and content)
  • Checklists
  • Labels
  • Projects
  • Reminders
  • Attachments such as images and other files you add to your notes.
  • Settings, including your theme preferences and app lock PIN (stored securely).

This data resides solely on your device.

b. Permissions

NoteNext requests certain permissions to enable its features. All permissions are used only for the stated purpose within the app's functionality:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: To allow you to attach images and other files from your device's storage to your notes.
  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To fetch link previews, download Google Fonts, and perform Google Drive backups (if enabled).
  • USE_BIOMETRIC: To enable the App Lock feature, allowing you to secure the app with your fingerprint or other biometric credentials.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM & USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT: To set and display precise reminders that you create for your notes.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To show notifications for your reminders.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC: To ensure reliable data backups to Google Drive, preventing the system from interrupting the upload process during long-running operations.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide and enhance your experience with NoteNext. This includes:

  • Storing, displaying, and organizing your notes and other content.
  • Enabling features like reminders, app lock, and rich link previews.
  • Personalizing your experience through themes and layout settings.
  • Data Backup: If you choose to use the Google Drive Backup feature, your data is securely zipped and uploaded to your personal Google Drive account.

All data processing occurs locally on your device, unless you explicitly choose to backup your data to Google Drive.

3. Google Drive Backup

NoteNext offers an optional feature to backup your notes to Google Drive. If you choose to use this feature:

  • Authentication: You will be asked to sign in with your Google account.
  • Restricted Access: The app requests access only to the files it creates (using the drive.file and drive.appdata scopes). It cannot read or access any other files in your Google Drive.
  • Data Handling: Your notes and attachments are compressed into a standard zip file and uploaded directly to your Google Drive. We do not have access to this data, nor is it sent to any third-party servers other than your own Google Drive storage.
  • User Control: You have full control to link or unlink your account and delete your backups at any time.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

I do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Since all your data is stored locally on your device (or in your personal Google Drive if backup is enabled), I do not have access to it. The only exception is when you voluntary choose to share a note using the app's "Share" functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

NoteNext uses a few third-party libraries to provide its features:

  • Google Drive API: Used strictly for the optional backup and restore functionality.
  • Google Fonts: To provide a better visual experience, the app may download fonts from Google Fonts.
  • Jsoup: To generate rich link previews, the app makes a network request to the URL you provide in a note.

These services have their own privacy policies, and I encourage you to review them. NoteNext does not send any of your personal note content to these services (except for the user-initiated Google Drive backup).

6. Data Security, Retention, and Deletion

I value your trust in providing us your Personal Information. I have implemented the following measures to protect it:

  • Local Storage: All your note data is stored in a private, local database on your device.
  • Secure Backup: Cloud backups are stored in your personal Google Drive, protected by your Google Account credentials.
  • App Lock: You can enable an additional layer of security by setting up a PIN or using biometric authentication to protect access to the app.

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you use the app. You can delete notes at any time, which moves them to the Bin. Uninstalling the app will also delete all of its local data. You can manually delete your Google Drive backups through the app settings or via Google Drive directly.
