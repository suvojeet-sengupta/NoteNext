# Widgets

Access your notes instantly from your home screen.

## NoteNext Widget

A simple, scrollable list of your recent notes.
- **Preview**: See note titles and partial content.
- **Quick Action**: Tap a note in the widget to open it directly in the app.
- **Add Note**: Tap the **+** button on the widget header to jump straight to the "Create Note" screen.

### How to Add
1. Long-press on your Home Screen.
2. Select **Widgets**.
3. Scroll down to **NoteNext**.
4. Drag and drop the widget onto your screen.
5. Resize as needed.
