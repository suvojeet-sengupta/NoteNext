# Backup & Restore

Data safety is a priority in NoteNext. We offer robust options to secure your data and move it between devices.

## â˜ï¸ Google Drive Backup

NoteNext allows you to back up your encrypted data directly to your own Google Drive.
- **Why Google Drive?**: It keeps your data under your control. We do not have our own servers; your data lives where you live.
- **Sync**: Perform manual backups to upload your current database.
- **Restore**: Restore from a previous Google Drive backup to retrieve lost notes or migrate to a new device.

### Setup
1. Go to **Settings > Backup & Restore**.
2. Tap **Sign in with Google**.
3. Once authenticated, tap **Backup Now**.

Create a physical backup file (`.zip`) on your device's storage.
- **Export**: Generates a ZIP file containing your notes and database.
- **Import**: Restore from a previously exported ZIP file.
- **Use Case**: Useful for manual archiving or transferring data via USB/File Share without cloud access.

## ðŸ“¥ Import from Google Keep

Transitioning from Google Keep? We've made it easy.
1. **Export from Google**: Go to [Google Takeout](https://takeout.google.com/), deselect everything, and select **Keep**. Download the export (ZIP format).
2. **Import to NoteNext**:
    - Go to **Settings > Backup & Restore**.
    - Tap **Import Notes > Google Keep**.
    - Select your downloaded Google Takeout ZIP file.
3. **Result**: Your notes, labels, colors, and checklists are preserved and imported into NoteNext.
