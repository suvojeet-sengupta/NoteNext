## üì± App Overview

**NoteNext** is designed to be:
- **Minimal & Beautiful**: A clean, distraction-free UI.
- **Privacy-First**: Offline-first architecture. Your data stays on your device unless you choose to back it up to your own Google Drive.
- **Open Source**: Transparent development and community-driven.
